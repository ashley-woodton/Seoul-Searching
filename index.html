About:

Welcome to Seoul, the modernly ancient capital of South Korea. You can find skyscrapers, designer stores, and neon signs everywhere. Though once you turn the corner, you'll find night markets and ancient palaces and temples. You won't only find a bustling city and ancient sites, but you can also find plenty of free public green spaces to just observe nature. With so much variety in activities, why wouldn't Seoul be the perfect vacation destination?

Fun Facts:

  1. Seoul is the capital of South Korea.
  2. It is the largest city in the country.
  3. The city is bisected by the Han River.
  4. Seoul is borderd by 8 mountains.
  5. Sometimes locals will say "kimchi" instead of cheese when taking photos.
  6. Tipping is not required in Seoul.
  7. Seoul is home of the world's larget indoor theme park.
  8. The Gwangjang Market is a traditional street market in Seoul with over 5,000 shops.
  9. Seoul is home of the Banpo Grand Bridge which goes over the Han River and is the lingest bridge fountain in the world.
